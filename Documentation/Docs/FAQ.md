## Getting Started

### What is Swiff?

Swiff is a beautiful, privacy-focused subscription tracker and expense manager for iPhone and iPad. Track all your subscriptions, get smart reminders, visualize spending, and share costs with friends - all without creating an account.

### Is Swiff really free?

Yes! Swiff is 100% free with all core features included:
- Unlimited subscriptions
- Unlimited transactions
- Unlimited people
- Smart notifications
- Analytics and charts
- Backup and export
- No ads, ever

Future premium features (like cloud sync across devices and custom themes) may have optional pricing, but subscription tracking will always be free.

### Do I need to create an account?

Nope! Swiff works entirely on your device. No account, no login, no password, no email required. Just download and start tracking.

### What devices does Swiff support?

- iPhone (iOS 16.0 or later)
- iPad (iPadOS 16.0 or later)
- Works on: iPhone SE, iPhone 12/13/14/15/16, all iPad models

Mac and Apple Watch support coming in future updates.

## Privacy & Security

### Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored securely on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's built-in encrypted database (SwiftData). Nothing is sent to our servers because we don't have any servers!

### Does Swiff send my data anywhere?

No. Zero data leaves your device (currently). Your subscription information, financial data, and personal details stay 100% private on your device.

### Can I encrypt my data?

Yes! When creating backups, you can optionally protect them with password encryption. Device storage is already encrypted by iOS.

### Will you ever sell my data?

Never. We don't collect your data, so there's literally nothing to sell. Your privacy is our highest priority.

### What data does Swiff collect for analytics?

None. We don't use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have no idea how you use the app - and we like it that way.

### Can I lock the app with Face ID or Touch ID?

Coming in the next update! Biometric authentication and PIN lock are in development.

## Subscriptions

### How many subscriptions can I track?

Unlimited! Track 5 subscriptions or 500 - Swiff handles it all.

### Can Swiff automatically detect my subscriptions?

Not yet. You manually add each subscription. We're exploring automatic detection from email receipts and bank connections in future versions, but manual tracking ensures maximum privacy.

### What subscription types are supported?

All of them! Common categories include:
- Streaming (Netflix, Spotify, Disney+, etc.)
- Software (Adobe, Microsoft 365, Notion, etc.)
- Fitness (Gym memberships, fitness apps)
- News & Media (Newspapers, magazines)
- Gaming (Xbox Game Pass, PlayStation Plus)
- Cloud Storage (iCloud, Dropbox, Google Drive)
- Education (Coursera, MasterClass)
- And any custom category you create

### Can I track free trials?

Absolutely! Swiff has dedicated free trial tracking:
- Mark subscription as "Free Trial"
- Set trial end date
- Get alerts 3 days, 1 day, and on expiration day
- Automatic conversion to paid tracking

### What if my subscription price changes?

Just update the price in Swiff. It will:
- Automatically save the old price
- Create a price history record
- Show price change chart
- Alert you to price increases
- Track percentage changes

You can see full price history for any subscription.

### Can I track subscriptions billed annually?

Yes! Swiff supports all billing cycles:
- Weekly
- Bi-weekly
- Monthly
- Quarterly (every 3 months)
- Bi-annually (every 6 months)
- Annually (yearly)
- Custom intervals (e.g., every 45 days)

### How do shared subscriptions work?

1. Add a subscription normally
2. Tap "Share Subscription"
3. Select people to share with
4. Choose split method:
   - Equal split (e.g., $15 Ã· 3 people = $5 each)
   - Custom amounts (e.g., you pay $10, friend pays $5)
   - Percentage-based (e.g., 60% you, 40% them)
5. Swiff tracks who owes what automatically

### What's the difference between Pause and Cancel?

- **Pause**: Subscription is still active, but you stop tracking renewals temporarily. Use for subscriptions you've paused but might resume.
- **Cancel**: Subscription is cancelled, no more renewals or reminders. Use when you've actually cancelled the service.
- **Delete**: Completely removes subscription from Swiff. Can't be undone unless you have a backup.

## Notifications & Reminders

### What types of notifications does Swiff send?

- **Renewal Reminders**: Before subscriptions renew
- **Trial Expiration**: When free trials are ending
- **Price Increase Alerts**: When subscription costs go up
- **Payment Reminders**: For pending payments to people
- **Unused Subscriptions**: Services you haven't used in X days

### When do I get reminded about renewals?

You choose! Set reminders:
- 1 day before renewal
- 3 days before (recommended default)
- 7 days before
- 14 days before
- 30 days before
- Custom number of days

You can set different reminder times for different subscriptions.

### Can I customize notification times?

Yes! In Settings:
- Set preferred reminder time (e.g., 9:00 AM)
- Enable quiet hours (no notifications during specific times)
- Choose which types of notifications you want

### Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check these:

1. **System Permission**:
   - Go to iPhone Settings > Notifications > Swiff
   - Ensure "Allow Notifications" is ON
   - Check notification style is set

2. **In-App Settings**:
   - Open Swiff > Settings
   - Ensure "Enable Notifications" toggle is ON
   - Check specific notification types are enabled

3. **Subscription Settings**:
   - Open subscription detail
   - Verify "Enable Reminders" is ON
   - Check reminder days are configured

4. **Send Test Notification**:
   - Settings > Notifications > "Send Test Notification"
   - Should receive test immediately

### Can I snooze notifications?

Coming soon! Current version lets you dismiss notifications. Future updates will add snooze options.

## Transactions & Expenses

### What's the difference between subscriptions and transactions?

- **Subscriptions**: Recurring payments that happen automatically (Netflix, Spotify, etc.)
- **Transactions**: One-time expenses or income (coffee purchase, paycheck, dinner with friends)

Swiff tracks both!

### Can I link transactions to subscriptions?

Yes! When adding a transaction, you can link it to a subscription. This helps:
- See all charges from a service
- Verify subscription cost matches actual charges
- Track additional fees or taxes

### How do I add a receipt?

1. Open any transaction
2. Tap "Edit"
3. Tap "Add Receipt"
4. Take photo or choose from library
5. Receipt is stored with transaction

Receipts are compressed and stored securely on device.

### Can I categorize transactions?

Absolutely! Swiff includes 14 built-in categories:
- Entertainment
- Subscriptions
- Groceries
- Transportation
- Utilities
- Healthcare
- Education
- Shopping
- Dining
- Travel
- Fitness
- Business
- Personal
- Other

You can also create custom categories.

### How do I export transactions?

Settings > Export Data > Choose format:
- **CSV**: Open in Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers
- **JSON**: Complete data with all fields

You can export:
- All data
- Date range
- Specific categories
- Selected transactions only (using bulk select)

## People & Sharing

### Who are "People" in Swiff?

People are individuals you share expenses or subscriptions with - friends, family, roommates, etc.

### How do I add a person?

1. Go to People tab
2. Tap "Add Person"
3. Enter name (required) and email (optional)
4. Optionally link to iOS Contacts
5. Save

### What's a "balance"?

Balance shows the net amount between you and another person:
- **Green (+$25)**: They owe you $25
- **Red (-$15)**: You owe them $15
- **Gray ($0.00)**: All settled up

### How do I split an expense?

1. Add a transaction
2. Select "Split with People"
3. Choose people to split with
4. Choose split method (equal, custom, percentage)
5. Swiff calculates each person's share

### Can I request payment through Swiff?

Yes!
1. Open person detail
2. Tap "Request Payment"
3. Enter amount and note
4. Share via Messages, Email, WhatsApp, etc.

The message includes payment details and your preferred payment method (Venmo, PayPal, etc.)

### How do I settle a balance?

1. Open person detail
2. Tap "Settle Balance"
3. Choose:
   - "They paid me" (reduces their debt)
   - "I paid them" (reduces your debt)
4. Amount defaults to current balance
5. Transaction recorded automatically

### Can I link to iOS Contacts?

Yes! When adding a person:
1. Tap "Link to Contact"
2. Search your contacts
3. Select contact
4. Name, email, phone auto-fill
5. Quick access to call/message from Swiff

## Backup & Data

### Why should I create backups?

Backups protect your data:
- Before app updates
- Before switching devices
- If you accidentally delete something
- Peace of mind!

We recommend monthly backups.

### How do I create a backup?

1. Settings > Data Management
2. Tap "Create Backup"
3. Choose location:
   - Files app (local device)
   - iCloud Drive
   - Any cloud service
   - Email to yourself
4. Backup file (.json) is created

### How do I restore from backup?

1. Settings > Data Management
2. Tap "Import/Restore Data"
3. Select backup file (.json)
4. Choose conflict resolution:
   - Keep existing data (skip duplicates)
   - Replace with backup (overwrite)
   - Merge (keep both)
5. Data is restored

### Can I restore on a different device?

Yes! Transfer the backup .json file to the new device (via AirDrop, email, cloud storage, etc.), then import it. All your data transfers completely.

### What if I lose my backup file?

Unfortunately, if the backup is lost and you've deleted the app or cleared data, it can't be recovered. Always keep multiple backup copies in different locations!

### What's included in a backup?

Everything:
- All subscriptions
- All transactions
- All people
- All relationships and links
- All notes and attachments
- Settings and preferences

Basically, a complete snapshot of your Swiff data.

## Analytics & Charts

### What analytics does Swiff provide?

- **Spending Trends**: Line chart showing spending over time
- **Category Breakdown**: Pie chart of spending by category
- **Top Subscriptions**: Bar chart of most expensive services
- **Monthly Comparison**: This month vs last month
- **Year-over-Year**: Compare to same month last year
- **Forecasting**: Predict future spending based on trends
- **Insights**: Smart recommendations to save money

### How accurate are spending forecasts?

Forecasts use linear regression on your historical data. Accuracy improves with more data:
- 1 month of data: Rough estimate
- 3 months: Moderate accuracy
- 6+ months: Quite accurate

Forecasts assume spending patterns continue. Life changes (new job, moving, etc.) affect accuracy.

### What are "savings opportunities"?

Swiff analyzes your subscriptions and suggests ways to save:
- **Unused subscriptions**: Services you haven't used in 30+ days
- **Annual discounts**: Subscriptions that offer annual plans with savings
- **Price increases**: Services that recently increased prices
- **Cheaper alternatives**: Similar services that cost less (manual suggestions)

### Can I export analytics?

Not directly, but you can:
- Take screenshots of charts
- Export raw data (CSV/JSON) and create charts in Excel/Sheets
- Future: PDF report export coming

## Technical & Troubleshooting

### App keeps crashing. What do I do?

1. **Update to latest version** (App Store)
2. **Restart your device** (hold power button, slide to power off)
3. **Free up storage** (Settings > General > iPhone Storage)
4. **Last resort**: Delete and reinstall (create backup first!)

If issues persist, email support@swiffapp.com with device model and iOS version.

### Data isn't saving. Help!

Try these:

1. Force close Swiff and reopen
2. Check available storage (need 50MB+ free)
3. Restart device
4. If corrupted, restore from backup

### Import fails with error. Why?

Common causes:

1. **Wrong file format**: Must be .json for backups, .csv or .json for exports
2. **Corrupted file**: Try re-downloading or using different backup
3. **File permissions**: Ensure Swiff can access Files app
4. **Large file**: Files over 100MB may be slow to import

### App is slow/laggy. How to fix?

If you have 1000+ transactions:

1. Archive old data (export and delete transactions over 1 year old)
2. Clear cache (Settings > Data Management > Clear Cache - coming soon)
3. Close background apps
4. Restart device

Swiff is optimized for thousands of transactions, but very large datasets may slow older devices.

### Can I use Swiff on iPad?

Yes! Swiff is fully optimized for iPad with larger layouts and iPad-specific features.

### Does Swiff work offline?

Yes! Everything works offline since all data is on-device. You only need internet for:
- Downloading the app
- Future: Cloud sync (when available)
